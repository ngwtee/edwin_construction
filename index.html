Our services

Seal Coating

We help to prevent issues like cracking on your asphalt surfacing by sealcoating it. Sealcoating creates a membrane that protects the usphalt or other substrate from the damaging effects of the sun, vehicle fluids, rock salt, oil, and water. This protects and prolongs surface's life expectancy.

Asphalt Maintenance

Asphalt maintenance plans can vary in scope, but they never vary in their purpose - to increase pavement life. A proper asphalt maintenance program includes crack filling, pothole repair, sealcoating, and many other surface applications, depending on the condition of the surface.

Concrete Services

Whether you need new concrete poured or old concrete repaired, choose only us - the most skilled and reliable. Options range from a plain gray surface, to beautiful decorative concrete finishes with pattern and texture. We also offer: Concrete cleaning, cutting and removal services

Residential Paving

We do braai areas, patios, pools, commercial and industrial paving uniquely to add definition to your outdoor space in subtle. With so many design options available we are able to give you exactly what you require. We also do repairs in the event of damage to your current paving.

Road construction

Experienced in the construction of a new roadway or the conversion of an existing unpaved road to a paved road or road maintenance on already existing roads. We do all kind of roads, including but limited to; Earthen roads, Gravel Roads, Bituminous roads, Concrete roads

Walkways & Driveways

Concrete and asphalt are by far the most popular surfaces for driveways, however we also use driveway pavers such as Concrete Pavers, Brick Pavers and Cobblestones which feature a variety of paver shapes that can create your unique design to add to your landscape setting.
